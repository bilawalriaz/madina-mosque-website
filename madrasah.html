Madrasah

Islamic Education for All Ages

We Strive To Provide Islamic Education and become a Centre of Islamic Educational Excellence in Greater Manchester.

Aims & Objectives

1

To help students appreciate Qur'anic texts with confidence and proper recitation

2

To teach basic Islamic injunctions according to the Hanafi Madhab

3

To prepare students to live as Muslims with Tawheed at the core

4

To promote dignity through self-respect and respect for others

5

To ensure equal access for all students

6

To work closely with parents in supporting their children

7

To provide a caring, secure environment with discipline and respect

8

To provide a joyous Islamic atmosphere where students enjoy learning

Our Programs

Quran Recitation

Learn to read and recite the Holy Quran with proper tajweed and pronunciation

Hifz Program

Memorize the Holy Quran with dedicated teachers and supportive environment

Islamic Studies

Comprehensive Islamic education including fiqh, hadith, and seerah
